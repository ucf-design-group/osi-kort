Knights of the RoundTable is an advisory council that serves as an educational resource to all student organizations and as a liaison between the organizations and the UCF community. We can help connect you to one of over 400 diverse registered student organizations on campus. If there is an organization we don't have that you want, we can help you start your own registered student organization!
