KnightQuest

Interested in getting involved on campus? Knights of the RoundTable is introducing a new service called KnightQuest, which is designed to help students become more involved at UCF. KnightQuest is a one-on-one meeting with a KoRT representative, who will personally introduce you to all the various ways in which a Knight can get involved at our university. Start your quest for success now by contacting one of our KnightQuest coordinators.
